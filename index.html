About Music Therapy

Music is an immensly powerful medium that can be found everywhere in nature. It also unlocks the amazing potential to alter the human body's response to certain conditions, without having to use traditional medicine or therapies. The healing power of music has opened up novel vistas of remedies to people suffering from premature infants, to the geriatric population suffering from Alzheimer's disease, autism, or stress and anxiety.

About Me

Siddhant Sanjay

My name is Siddhant Sanjay and I have been practicing the piano for the past 11 years. Playing the piano started as a hobby and then became a passion. Playing piano has helped me physically and spiritually. Over the years, I have performed publicly on numerous occasions like chruches during the Holidays or at events like Mother's Day. Performing in front of people helped me grow as a person and as an artist. It imbibed patience in me and opened the doors to new horizons.


When I delved deeper into playing piano, I realized the potential music has in healing. Exploreing the healing properties of Piano based music, I started Music Therapy and now I want to share my Music therapy experience with others through Feather Music.
